# Caio Launch Copy

## LinkedIn

I just launched Caio: a cleaner search engine for tech jobs.

The first version is intentionally simple: public job postings, normalized into a faster search experience with better company, location, salary, and source signals.

The bigger idea is the next layer: an application agent that can help candidates match jobs, tailor their CV, and apply consistently without spending hours on repetitive forms.

For now, the job search is free and open source.

https://caio-jobs.com
